Jury Rights?

  • The right to a trial by jury for non petty offenses. However a citizen may waive the right to agree to trail an dappear before a judge. The court when in the presence of a jury may not usurp the jury sfunction by directing a guilty verdict, or attempt to interfere with the jurors independent judgment. These jurors must be selected from a pool that represents a fair cross section of the community and be unbiased.
